Process Description

1. Client Registration: New clients sign up through the company-specific registration form, selecting their preferred salon location.
2. Data Validation: System validates client information (email, phone, name) and ensures uniqueness within the company.
3. Template Selection: System checks for company-specific welcome email templates first, falls back to global template, or creates default content.
4. Email Customization: Template is customized with client-specific information (name, company details, login credentials).
5. Email Delivery: Welcome email is sent to client's email address with salon branding and next steps.
6. Status Tracking: System tracks email delivery status and updates client record accordingly.

Key Components

  • Client Model: Stores client information with company_id association
  • EmailCampaign Model: Stores welcome email templates scoped by company
  • Company Scoping: Each company has its own welcome email templates
  • Template Inheritance: Falls back to global template if company-specific not found
  • Email Service: Handles actual email delivery and tracking
